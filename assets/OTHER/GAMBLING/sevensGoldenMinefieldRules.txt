
*Place a bet and a twentieth of the bet will become your base return
*Seven cards in order of ascending value will be placed in front of you
*You can choose to 'retreat' causing you to take your total return without making another move
*You can choose to 'press' and take flip one card from low to high
*The value of this card is multiplied by the base return and added to the total return
*If you flip a face card you will instantlly lose money proportionate to (base return-bet)
